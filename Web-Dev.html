Honest Scope

What This Service is Not For

We believe in being upfront. This service is the right fit for most service businesses — but not every use case.

🛒

Online Stores / Ecommerce

If you need product listings, a shopping cart, and payment processing — this isn't the right service. We'd recommend Shopify for that use case.

🔐

Membership Areas & Logins

Password-protected member portals, gated content, or user accounts are outside the scope of a pure HTML build.

📅

Booking & Scheduling Systems

Built-in appointment booking or availability calendars require back-end logic. We'd point you to Cal.com or Calendly for this.

This service is ideal for: service businesses, consultants, personal brands, local businesses, and startups that need a fast, professional website to generate leads and build credibility — without the ongoing cost and complexity of a CMS platform.
