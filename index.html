Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Jax.

Founder — Prism Performance Marketing

I started Prism Performance Marketing after years of seeing how much businesses leave on the table when marketing is done wrong. Before founding PPM, I trained Meta advertisers from within the official Meta advertising programme — then led APAC product training at TikTok across Southeast Asia. That's where I learned how these platforms actually work, not just how they appear to work from the outside.

Today I partner with SMEs across Australia, Singapore, Malaysia and international markets to build ad strategies that convert. Whether you're a solo founder or a scaling team, my focus is turning your ad spend into qualified leads, measurable online sales and sustainable long-term growth.
